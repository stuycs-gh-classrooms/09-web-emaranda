Comics Publication

Ms. Marvel was created by artist Adrian Alphona, editor Sana Amanat, and writer G. Willow Wilson. Kamala's first appearance was in the background of Captain Marvel #14, and her first full appearance was in All-New Marvel Now! Point One #1. Ms. Marvel's solo series's first issue was published in February 2014. The second Volume of the Ms. Marvel series was published from 2015 to 2018. A new series titled The Magnificent Ms. Marvel was published from 2019 to 2021. Ms. Marvel has also appeared in many limited series and cameos in the solo series of other Marvel Superheros. Ms. Marvel's series was very successful. Ms. Marvel: No Normal won the Hugo Award for best graphic story in 2015. Ms. Marvel: No Normal (Volume 1) was a best-selling graphic novel in 2014, and debuted high on the New York Times Best Seller list of paperback graphic novels.
