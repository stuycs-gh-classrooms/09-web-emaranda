Villains

The Inventor

The Inventor is the first main antagonist in the Ms. Marvel series (Generation Why). When attempting to create a clone of Thomas Edison, the bird of scientist Gregory Knox was caught in the procedure. This create the Inventor, a half bird man with the brain of Thomas Edison. The Inventor is the main antagonist of the first Ms. Marvel comics, where Ms. Marvel is still learning to control her powers. The Inventor uses people in order to power his inventions. These people are volunteers who believe that their life is useless, and powering a machine is the only way to make themselves useful in the world.

Kamran

Kamran is a family friend of the Khans, and when Kamala first meets him, she immediately develops a crush. He seems to be perfect: incredibly smart, likes the same Bollywood movies, and plays the same video game World of Battlecraft. However, when Kamran offers to take Kamala to school, he kidnaps her and knocks her out. He takes her to New Attilan because he wants all of the Inhumans to rise up and take over the world.
